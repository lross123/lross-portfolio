Tutoring Business

For several years I managed a small tutoring business, teaching mathematics, statistics and programming to students ranging from high school to postgraduate level. This experience not only honed my communication skills but also deepened my understanding of the foundational concepts that underpin data science and computer science.

Services Offered

  • Mathematics tutoring (calculus, linear algebra, probability, discrete maths)
  • Programming tutorials (Python, R, MATLAB)
  • Data analysis and visualisation workshops
  • Exam preparation and study strategies

Highlights

During my time as a tutor I achieved the following:

  • Helped over 50 students improve their grades by an average of one letter grade.
  • Developed customised lesson plans tailored to individual learning styles.
  • Built a network of repeat clients through word‑of‑mouth recommendations.
  • Received consistently positive feedback for clarity and patience.

I continue to enjoy teaching and mentoring others, and I am always open to opportunities to share knowledge and support aspiring data scientists.
